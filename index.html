About the Project

Coursera Study Buddy helps you interact with Coursera transcripts using AI to:

  • Summarize course content
  • Ask questions about the material
  • Generate practice quizzes
  • Prepare for exams

Features

  • Upload and Process Transcripts: Easily upload Coursera PDF transcripts
  • Generate Summaries: Get concise summaries of course material
  • Q&A: Ask questions about the content and get AI-powered answers
  • Quiz Creation: Automatically generate quizzes to test your knowledge
  • Exam Preparation: Tools to help you prepare for assessments
